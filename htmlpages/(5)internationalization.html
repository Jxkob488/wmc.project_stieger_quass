Internationalization

Internationalization prepares an online shop to operate in multiple countries and markets. It allows content, prices and legal settings to adapt to different regions.

  • Multilingual content and navigation
  • Support for multiple currencies
  • Localization of formats and units
  • Country-specific tax and legal rules
  • International payment and shipping options
