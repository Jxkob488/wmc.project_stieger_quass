Overview: Cost Structure

Online shop systems follow different cost models depending on technology, hosting approach, and the target business size...

Typical Cost Categories

  • Platform or license fees: monthly subscriptions or yearly licenses.
  • Hosting costs: from shared hosting to cloud environments.
  • Theme and design costs
  • Plugins and extensions

Example price ranges

  • SaaS: €20–€300 monthly
  • Self‑hosted: €5–€40 hosting
  • Enterprise: €15k+
