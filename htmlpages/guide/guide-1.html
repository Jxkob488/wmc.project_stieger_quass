Before starting an online shop, it is important to understand what an online shop is and what role a shop system plays.

An online shop allows customers to browse products, add them to a shopping cart and complete purchases digitally.

A shop system is the technical foundation of an online shop. It manages products, orders, payments and customer data.

This guide explains step by step how to plan and create an online shop using modern shop systems.
