üß† Step 2: Define Your Shop Idea

Before choosing a shop system, the shop idea must be clearly defined. The type of product and the scope of the project influence all further decisions.

Online shops can sell physical products, digital products or services. Physical products require shipping, stock management and return handling, while digital products are usually delivered automatically.

It is also important to think about the size of the project. A small shop for a few products has different requirements than a shop that is planned to grow over time.

Defining the shop idea at this stage helps avoid choosing a shop system that is either too complex or too limited.
