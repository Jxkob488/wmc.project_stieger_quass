After selecting a shop system, the next important step is choosing the right hosting solution. Hosting determines where the shop runs and how fast and reliable it is.

Small shops often start with shared hosting because it is inexpensive and easy to set up. However, performance can be limited when traffic increases.

VPS and cloud hosting provide more resources and better performance. These options are suitable for growing shops and allow scaling when needed.

The hosting choice should match the expected traffic, budget and technical knowledge to avoid performance problems later.
