After setting up the design and structure of the shop, the next step is adding products. Products are the core of every online shop.

Each product should have a clear name, a detailed description and high-quality images. Good product information helps customers understand what they are buying.

Prices, taxes and shipping information must be defined correctly to avoid confusion during checkout.

Well-organized categories and product filters improve navigation and make it easier for customers to find what they are looking for.
