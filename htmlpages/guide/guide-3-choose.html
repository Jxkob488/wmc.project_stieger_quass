Choosing the right shop system is one of the most important steps when creating an online shop. It affects flexibility, costs and long-term maintenance.

Hosted shop systems are easy to use and manage. They handle hosting, updates and security automatically, but offer limited customization.

Self-hosted shop systems provide more control and flexibility. They allow deeper customization, but require technical knowledge and maintenance.

The best choice depends on the shop idea, available budget and long-term goals. A balanced decision helps avoid problems later.
