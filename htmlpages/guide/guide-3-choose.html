In this step, a concrete shop system is selected. The shop system is the software that is used daily to manage products, orders, customers and payments.

Different shop systems are used depending on the type and size of the shop. Small shops often use hosted systems, while growing businesses prefer self-hosted solutions with more flexibility.

Examples of commonly used shop systems include Shopify, WooCommerce, Shopware and Magento. A comparison and overview of these systems can be found here: Shop Systems Examples.

Choosing a shop system means deciding how much control, flexibility and technical responsibility is required. The selected system should fit the shop idea, the budget and future plans.
