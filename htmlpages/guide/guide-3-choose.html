🛠️ Step 3: Choose Your Shop System

To find the right system, you first need to be honest about your situation. Different projects require different tools, and picking the wrong one can lead to a lot of frustration later on.

If you are an individual with no prior experience, you should look for a system that is easy to handle and ready to go. If you are an experienced individual who wants to build something unique, you might prefer a system that gives you more freedom to experiment. For large companies with thousands of products and complex needs, professional enterprise solutions are the standard.

Choose the path that fits your current skills and goals. You can find more details on which system belongs to which category in our Overview.
