Hosting Models

Hosting models describe how and where an online shop is hosted. They differ in terms of control, scalability, maintenance effort and cost. Choosing the right model depends on the size of the business and technical requirements.

SaaS solutions are fully hosted platforms. The provider takes care of servers, updates and security.

Examples: Shopify, BigCommerce,

Advantages: fast setup, low maintenance
Disadvantages: limited customization, ongoing fees

PaaS provides a platform for developers to build and deploy applications without managing the underlying infrastructure.

Examples: Heroku, Google App Engine

Advantages: scalable, developer-friendly
Disadvantages: platform dependency

IaaS offers virtual servers and infrastructure with full control over the environment.

Examples: AWS EC2, Microsoft Azure

Advantages: maximum flexibility, high scalability
Disadvantages: higher technical complexity
