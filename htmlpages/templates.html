Overview: Templates & Themes

What are templates?

Templates define the visual layout and design of an online shop. They control how product pages, navigation, headers, footers and content elements appear to the user. Most shop systems offer a wide variety of templates ranging from simple free designs to complex, customizable premium themes.

Types of Templates

  • Free templates: simple and easy to use, limited customization.
  • Premium templates: high‑quality designs with extended features.
  • Custom templates: created by developers, maximum flexibility.
  • Theme builders: drag‑and‑drop tools for non‑technical users.

Examples from Popular Platforms

  • Shopify Theme Store – modern, responsive templates.
  • Wix Templates – easy beginner-friendly builder.
  • WooCommerce Themes – flexible for developers.
  • Shopware Themes – professional and scalable.

Advantages of Using Templates

  • Fast shop setup
  • Professional design without coding
  • Responsive layouts for all devices
  • Often customizable through theme settings

Disadvantages of Templates

  • Limited flexibility compared to custom designs
  • Popular templates may look similar across shops
  • Premium themes can be expensive
  • Custom changes may require coding knowledge

When should you use a template?

Templates are ideal for beginners, small businesses, and projects that need a fast and cost‑effective start. For highly unique branding, custom development may be the better option.
