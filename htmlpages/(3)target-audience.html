Which Shop System Fits Your Business?

Different shop systems are designed for different types of businesses. The right choice depends on company size, technical skills, budget and individual requirements.

Target Group Example Systems Main Advantage Main Disadvantage
Small Businesses Shopify, Wix Easy to use Limited flexibility
Developers WooCommerce, PrestaShop Full control Needs technical skills
Medium Businesses Shopware, BigCommerce Scalable Higher cost
Enterprises SAP Commerce, Salesforce Commerce Cloud Very powerful Expensive & complex
