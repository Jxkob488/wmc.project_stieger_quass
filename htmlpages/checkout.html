Checkout Process

What is the checkout process?

The checkout process is the final step before a customer completes their order. A smooth and fast checkout is extremely important — every second counts, because complicated steps can lead to cart abandonment.

Main Checkout Steps

  1. Shopping Cart Review: customers confirm products and quantities.
  2. Customer Information: personal data and email address.
  3. Shipping Details: address and delivery options.
  4. Payment Method: selection of credit card, PayPal, Klarna, etc.
  5. Order Review: final summary before submitting.
  6. Confirmation Page: order number + email confirmation.

Good Practices

  • Use a one‑page checkout to reduce clicks.
  • Offer guest checkout without mandatory account creation.
  • Keep the design clean with minimal distractions.
  • Auto‑fill fields (address suggestions, saved payment methods).
  • Show progress indicators (e.g. Step 1 → Step 2 → Step 3).
  • Ensure mobile‑friendly fields and buttons.
