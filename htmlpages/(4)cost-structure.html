Cost Structure

The cost structure of an online shop depends on the selected shop system, hosting model and required features. Costs can be fixed or variable and usually increase with business size.

  • Platform & Licenses: monthly or yearly fees
  • Hosting: depends on traffic and performance needs
  • Themes & Design: free to custom-built solutions
  • Plugins & Extensions: additional functionality
  • Maintenance & Support: updates and security
