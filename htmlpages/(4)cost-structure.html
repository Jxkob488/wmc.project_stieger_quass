Cost Structure

The cost structure of an online shop depends on the chosen shop system, hosting model and required features. Costs can be fixed or variable and often grow with business size and complexity.

Some shop systems charge monthly or yearly license fees. These costs often depend on the selected plan and included features.

Examples: SaaS platforms like Shopify usually offer different subscription levels.

Typical range: €20 – €300 per month

Hosting costs vary depending on traffic, performance requirements and the hosting model (shared, cloud, dedicated).

Self-hosted systems like WooCommerce or Shopware require separate hosting.

Typical range: €5 – €100+ per month

Many shop systems offer free themes, but premium designs often provide better customization and support.

Custom designs created by agencies or developers significantly increase costs.

Typical range: €0 – €3,000+

Plugins extend shop functionality, such as payment methods, marketing tools or SEO features.

Many plugins require monthly subscriptions or license fees.

Typical range: €0 – €200+ per month

Ongoing costs include updates, backups, security checks and technical support.

For larger shops, professional maintenance is often outsourced.

Typical range: €50 – €500+ per month
